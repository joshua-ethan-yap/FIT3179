Organ Donations in Malaysia

Insights into Organ Donation Pledges and Household Income

In Malaysia, the number of people pledging their organs and participating in blood donations is closely tied to socio-economic factors. This data visualization explores organ donation pledges by state in 2023 and compares median household income with blood donations in 2022, offering insights into regional differences and how socio-economic factors may impact donor activity.
